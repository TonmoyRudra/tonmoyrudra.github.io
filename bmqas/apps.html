Bengal Mobile QA Solution (BMQAS) Apps

Bengal Mobile QA Solution has built Enterprise-class Apps for its Fortune 500 customers. We have also built consumer-grade mobile apps downloadable from the App Store. And we have built Web Applications and ECommerce-enabled high volume WebSites.

If you have an idea for an app, our mobile app architects can make it a successful reality across all devices, from iPhone and iPad apps to Android phone and tablet apps and even BlackBerry apps. Based on our industry insight, experience and analysis, we will take your initial idea and help you develop it into a full-fledged innovative app that meets your use-cases to engage your users and drive downloads. Different iOS or Android devices have different form-factors and capabilities. We will also ensure the quality of your app, by testing on multiple devices.

We also have built cloud-based apps hosted on rackspace or Amazon cloud-based services. We have taken over responsibilities for handling the hosting complexities of these Apps, so that customers can focus on their business challenges. Finally we have also built rich thick-client apps on the windows .NET/WPF platform.

Our App portfolio includes the following:

Supplementary Educational Services Attendance and Assessment reporting Web App

We have built and currently manage cloud-based Education Management Systems that provide reporting and management support for Supplemental Education Services to underprivileged students. The performance of tutors and Students spread across multiple school districts can be tracked through detailed assessment reports submitted to educational authorities.

Cloud-based Energy Management Business Intelligence Dashboard

This wizard-based dashboard allows corporate energy managers at multi-site retail or office operations to get a birds-eye-view of their ongoing operations and fine-tune their energy usage and expenses.

Mobile VoIP and Video-sharing Collaboration App

For one of our large Fortune-500 customers, we have built on iPad, iPhone and Android Tablet and Phone platforms including Samsung and HTC a collaboration application that embeds video sharing capabilities and audio conversations with a targeted audience.

Higher Education Kiosk-based Voting App

For the higher Education marketplace, we have built an iPad-based Kiosk Application where students, teachers and other community members can submit their votes and on a cloud-based portal Administrators can view the results as well as fine-tune the ongoing campaigns.

Blog and E-Commerce Site

For a renowned personality, we developed a high-volume site with regularly updated multi-lingual blogs and also ecommerce-integration, selling items to a global audience.

Cloud-based conference-proceedings publishing app

For a large state Department of Education initiative we built a cloud-based solution where editors can maintain the content for conference proceedings and then the documents can be printed out on-demand.

High Volume Email Notification System

We have built CRM Integration across internal IT customer databases and external notification systems for sending out on-time updates to improve customer satisfaction. For a direct marketing services provider, we created this as a browser-based plugin tool for their Contacts Management in a central repository
